What CareerReady AI does

The platform combines career matching, competency analysis, skills, tools, project evidence and readiness assessment in one system.

1

Career Matching

Compare your profile against Computer Science job roles and identify the roles that best match your skills.

2

Skill Gap Analysis

Detect missing skills and tools using role requirements and competency groups instead of treating every skill as mandatory.

3

Readiness Assessment

Measure academic and industry readiness for a selected role using your skills, tools and profile data.

4

Bottleneck Detection

Identify the main factor preventing employability, including skill, tool, project and readiness weaknesses.

5

Career Transition Analysis

Analyse how realistic a move from one Computer Science role to another is based on your current capabilities.

6

Project Portfolio

Record project evidence, mapped skills, tools, GitHub repositories and live project links.
